Experiment Note

Currently, the overall performance is very bad.

  1. We have already known the coverage problem of SDH, which is only good for identify around top 5% instead of top 30%.
  2. We tried to solve this by switching GUPS access pattern to a zipf distribution. We found the property of only identifying top 5% does not change with workload. In zipf, the top 5% key should make up more than 30% total accesses. However, there is not a visible change to end to end performance.
